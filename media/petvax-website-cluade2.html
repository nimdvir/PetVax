Our Vaccination Services

Comprehensive vaccine protection tailored to your pet's specific needs and lifestyle.

Dog Vaccination Services

Core Vaccines (Essential for all dogs)

Rabies

Legally required in New York. Protects against this fatal disease that can affect both pets and humans.

DHPPi Combination

Comprehensive protection against Distemper, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza, and Infectious Hepatitis.

Leptospirosis

Essential in New York where this bacterial infection is prevalent, commonly spread by urban wildlife like rats and mice.

Non-Core Vaccines (Lifestyle-based recommendations)

Bordetella (Kennel Cough)

Required for boarding and daycare. Recommended for dogs in contact with other dogs. Boost every 6 months for frequent dog park visitors.

Lyme Disease

Essential for dogs exposed to ticks during hiking, visits to parks, or time in natural areas with tall grass.

Canine Influenza

Recommended for dogs in boarding, daycare, or dog parks. Also advisable for dogs with heart or respiratory conditions.

Cat Vaccination Services

Rabies

Legally required in New York for all cats, including indoor-only cats. Essential protection against this fatal disease.

FVRCP Combination

Core protection against Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, and Panleukopenia (feline distemper).

Feline Leukemia Virus

Recommended for kittens regardless of lifestyle. Adult indoor cats typically don't need boosters, but outdoor cats should continue regular vaccination.

The Importance of Pet Vaccination

Disease Prevention

Vaccines expose your pet's immune system to harmless forms of disease agents, building immunity before exposure to the real threat.

Community Protection

Widespread vaccination helps protect all pets in the community by reducing disease transmission.

Cost-Effective Care

Preventing disease is much more affordable than treating serious illnesses that vaccines can prevent.

Legal Requirements

Many vaccines like rabies are legally required, and others may be necessary for boarding, daycare, or travel.
